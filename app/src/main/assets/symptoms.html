Leukemia Symptoms

Common Symptoms

  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Frequent or severe infections
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fever or chills
  • Easy bruising or bleeding (including nosebleeds)
  • Swollen lymph nodes, liver, or spleen
  • Bone pain or tenderness
  • Pale skin
  • Shortness of breath
